How Happy Times Delivery Works

Safe, Reliable, and on Demand

How did we get here?

During 2020, the social standards across Canada was to stay at home and limit the contact with others. That's when Good Times Delivery was created, to cut the stigma around these tough times we believe everyone can still have a Good Time.

How to Order

  1. Browse

    Good Times Delivery has hundreds of dispensaries and liquor stores to select from. When you first launch the app, you may browse for ideas or search for a certain brew or strain. Tap to add anything to your purchase when you see something you like.

  2. Order

    When you're ready to check out, you'll see your address as well as the total cost of your order, which includes tax and shipping. Simply hit Place order when everything appears to be in order, and you're done.

  3. Track

    In the app, keep track of your order. When your order is nearly ready, a delivery person will go to the store to pick it up and deliver it to you. You'll be able to view their name and photo on the map, as well as monitor their journey.
